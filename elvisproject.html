Logo Design

The creation of a compelling logo design inspired by the legendary Evlis Presley, aiming to encapsulate the essence of his iconic music, style, and charisma while maintaining a timeless and versatile aesthetic. The logo was designed for the application spanning from digital media and merchandise to signage, striking a balance between nostalgia for Elvis's era and contemmporary relevance, employing symbolic elements, retro typography, and a vibrant color palette.
