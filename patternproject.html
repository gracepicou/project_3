Pattern Study

In this creative endeavor, we embark on a captivating exploration of pattern design, where the familiar becomes extraordinary. Through a meticulous arrangement of multiple columns and rows of squares, we transform personally captured images into three distinct patterns. Each square becomes a canvas for unique alterations, a symphony of shifts that breathe new life into the mundane. The resulting patterns are a testament to the endless possibilities of design, where everyday images are transmuted into visually stimulating compositions, offering a fresh perspective on the world around us.
