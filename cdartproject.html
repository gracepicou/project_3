CD Prints

In this creative endeavor, we will craft three CD cover designs, each meticulously fashioned after Mac Miller's iconic albums: "Faces," "The Divine Feminine," and "Blue Slide Park." These designs pay homage to Mac Miller's diverse musical journey, evoking the essence, themes, and emotions of each album. The choice of different blue tones is not only a nod to Mac Miller's "Congratulations" with the lyric "you remind me of the color blue" but also symbolizes his emotional depth and versatility as an artist. "Faces" will feature dark, moody blues, reflecting its introspective and introspective nature; "The Divine Feminine" will embrace soft, calming blues, mirroring the album's romantic and soulful undertones; and "Blue Slide Park" will radiate vibrant, youthful blues, resonating with the album's nostalgia and optimism. These designs aim to encapsulate Mac Miller's artistic legacy through a harmonious blend of visual and lyrical storytelling.
